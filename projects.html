Solitude

Solitude is a meditation journal application. It allows users to keep track of the duration, moods, goal, and content of their meditation. If needed, users can also search up videos to help with their meditation such as guided meditation or a calm background sound like waterfall or rainfall. In order to use the functionality of the app, users need to register for an account and login.

ReciPro

ReciPro allow users to keep track of their pantry and fridge items as well as the items' expiration date. The app also allow users to create their own grocery list, search for recipes and save them to use it in the future. In order to use the functionality of the app, users need to register for an account and login.
